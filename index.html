Formats

Championship Match

The Championship Match consists of 14 games. First to 7.5 points wins. In case of a tie after 14 games:
Rapid: 4 games, 5 minutes + 5 seconds per player per move.
Blitz: 2 games, 3 minutes + 3 seconds per player per move.
Armageddon: 5 minutes for White and 4 minutes for Black. If the game ends in a draw, the player playing Black is declared the winner.
In this way, tiebreaks are played.

Candidates

It is played as playoffs with the logic first to 5 points depending on the number of participants. The winner gets the right to be an challenger for the Championship match.

Opens

The results are in the case of possible additional participants for Candidates. These are tournaments that determine who can and cannot participate, but have little to do with the actual championship. Usually played in 7 rounds or 14 rounds swiss format
